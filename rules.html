Ultimate Tournament Rules (2026)

Standard USAU 2026-2027 rules apply

Dates: June 8 - June 12, 2026

Round Robin Games

Monday, Tuesday, Wednesday (6/8, 6/9, 6/10)

  • Games to 11
  • Game start: 12:15
  • Soft Cap: 1:10
  • Hard Cap: 1:20
  • Half time at 6 points

Championship Game

Thursday (6/11)

  • Games to 13
  • Game start: 12:15
  • Soft Cap: 1:25
  • Hard Cap: 1:35
  • Half time at 12:50 (after that point ends)
  • Location: Rt-9 field

Game Rules

  • 1 timeout each half, 1 floating timeout
  • Start of game is a disc flip; winner chooses side or possession
  • Teams swap sides after halftime

Cap Rules

Soft Cap: When the horn blows, finish the point. If no winner at target score, add 1 goal to the leading team's score and play to that number.

Hard Cap: Finish the current point. The next point starts immediately. If one team leads, they win. If tied, play one final point.

Player & Conduct Rules

  • All players should be given time to play
  • Captains ensure fair participation
  • Dangerous plays: ejection on second violation
  • Injury replacement allowed with captain approval

Additional Notes

  • Makeup day: Friday (if needed)

Tie Breakers

  1. Win/Loss ratio
  2. Tied team point differential
  3. All teams point differential
