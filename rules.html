1 Timeout each half, 1 Floating Timeout

Start of game is a disc flip. Winner of the flip chooses side or possession. Teams swap after halftime.

When Soft cap horn blows, play until the end of the point. If there is no winner (at the original point target, win by 2 no longer applies), add 2 to the higher teamâ€™s score. Play until that new score is reached. If the new score is above 13, play until 13. Win by 2 no longer apples.

At hard cap, finish point currently in progress (if one team is on the line with 7 players, the point has started and will be played before determining the winner). If one team has more points, they win. If the teams are tied, play 1 more point to determine the winner.

In the spirit of Cognex and Ultimate, All players should be given time to be allowed to play. Captains should be sure that all players have a chance to play.

Truly dangerous plays results in Ejection from tournament on second violation.

Makeup day is Friday but hopefully not needed.

Tie breakers:

  1. Win/Loss ratio
  2. Tied Team Point differential
  3. All Teams point differential
